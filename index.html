Let me introduce my school

SMP Negeri 17 Palu is one of 24 junior high schools in Palu City. SMP Negeri 17 Palu is located in Pantoloan Village, Tawaeli District, in the Palu City Region in Central Sulawesi Province, which was built in 1983-1984, the building that was built consisted of 3 classrooms, a laboratory , 1 library, 1 room for skills, 1 room for office, and 1 room for administration. This school building was built on state land with an area of 20,052 m2. After going through construction for a year, in 1984 the school was officially opened.

SMP Negeri 17 Palu in 2021 has 15 study groups, consisting of 4 study groups for VII class, 5 study groups for VIII class, and 6 study groups for class IX. Each group averaged between 24 and 32 students, with a total of 453 students.
